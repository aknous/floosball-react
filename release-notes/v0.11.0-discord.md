# Floosball v0.11.0 — Personalities & Polish

Players are people now. Sort of.

## ✨ New

**🎭 Player Personalities**
Every player has a personality. Most are base vibes — Stoic, Chill, Fiery, Goofy, Wholesome, Cool, Lively, Melancholy, Unhinged. A small number are rare variants: Mystic, Alien, Prophet, Knight, Fossil, Ghost, Sleepwalker, Android, Time Traveler, and a few more. You'll see who's who by the way they react.

**📺 In-Game Reactions**
After touchdowns, field goals, sacks, interceptions, fumbles, turnovers on downs, clutch moments, and big WP swings, players react in-character in the play feed. Defenders get reactions too — sometimes the camera goes to the defender who got the sack instead of the QB who took it.

**🎬 Sideline Cutaways**
Between drives, at halftime, on big moments, the camera occasionally cuts to a sideline player mid-bit. A Goofy doing a magic trick. A Mystic drinking water with two hands. An Alien poking the turf in a small spiral pattern.

**🏆 Postgame Reactions**
Game ends — winners react positively, losers react negatively. Each team gets a few players talking after the final whistle.

**📊 Mood System**
Every player has a 1-5 mood that shifts with their recent performance. Mood names are personality-flavored: a Stoic might be "Composed" or "Locked In", a Mystic might be "Foreseeing" or "Threaded", a Chill is "Mellow" or "Cruising". Hover any player to see their current mood.

**📈 Card Score Projections**
Every equipped card now shows its projected output for the upcoming week — FP, FPx, Floobits, or a contextual status for amplifiers — based on your roster's season averages. Cards like Lemons compute their actual FP impact against your equipped hand.

## 🔧 Changes

- **Rebalanced card effects** — Possession dialed back, Lemons reworked, plus several other tuning passes.
- **Double Down is now Lemons.**
- **Highlights feed** simplified. Only scores, turnovers, and game start/end cards. Big-play markers and sideline cutaways now live in the per-game modal where you can dig in.
- Reaction text in the feed bumped to 13px so it's actually readable.
- Sideline cutaways in the modal show the player's team avatar.

## 🐛 Fixes

- Personality reactions now fire in playoff and Floos Bowl games (was regular season only).
- Reactions feel much less repetitive — picker uses a shuffled deck, so every line shows once before any repeats. Pools are 40+ lines per personality, so you shouldn't see the same reaction twice in a single game.
- Kneeling out the clock on a turnover on downs no longer duplicates in the play feed.
- Card descriptions and tooltips clarified — no more misleading static multipliers on cards whose actual output depends on hand state.
